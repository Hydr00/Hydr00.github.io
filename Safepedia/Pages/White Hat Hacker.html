How to become a white hat hacker

Technical demand

To become a white hat hacker, you must first have a strong technical appeal. There is a skill in the field of cryptography, mathematics, or IT. White Hat hackers are legitimate hackers whose real-life identities are security professionals whose job is to find, test and fix loopholes in crisis computers to make the wild Internet more secure. Among them are social engineering experts, network experts, hardware and software engineers, and there is no doubt that they have a deep understanding of computer technology.

There are some IT security or white hat hacker certifications in the world, and for those who are in urgent need of hacking experience, passing these certifications may be a shortcut to becoming a white hat hacker. For example, CEH certification, CEH is a neutral organization specializing in evaluating the technical skills of computer professionals, through its evaluation to prove that you have the qualifications to become a good white hat.

To be talented in this area, you need to have a comprehensive understanding of computer technology, enumeration, social engineering, SQL, Trojans, worms, and other forms of attacks, such as distributed denial attacks (DDoS), Candidates also have to participate in password testing, penetration testing, firewall testing and many other tests.

Moral restraint

After mastering comprehensive technology, moral requirements and considerations are also necessary for white hat hackers. Because the background of hackers exposed you to a lot of benefits and temptations, sometimes your behavior is on the edge of the law, which requires moral restraint. Make sure that your technology is a malicious attack for the benefit of mankind itself, not for personal gain.

If you feel that you are not well prepared for your skills or other levels, you can also take part in professional training. Learn how to restore deleted files, crack passwords, investigate and deal with network traffic, and use various forensics tools to collect information. You can also get CHFI credentials, etc., in short, over time, one day you will get what you want.

Finally, as a binding and self-controlled group of hackers, white hat hackers represent the pioneers of human technology, but also use their own technology to benefit mankind. It is the original intention of white hats to share the knowledge they have learned or mastered on the Internet to help more people solve problems.
