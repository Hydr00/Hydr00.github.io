LEVEL6-Identity authentication

Identity authentication technology is an effective solution to the process of confirming the identity of the operator in the computer network. All the information in the computer network world, including the identity information of the user, is represented by a specific set of data. The computer can only identify the digital identity of the user, and all the authorization to the user is also the authorization for the digital identity of the user. How to ensure that the operator who operates as a digital identity is the legitimate owner of this digital identity, that is to say, to ensure that the physical identity of the operator corresponds to the digital identity, the identity authentication technology is to solve this problem. As the first pass to protect network assets, identity authentication plays an important role.
