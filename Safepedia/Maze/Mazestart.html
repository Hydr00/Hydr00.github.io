Welcome to Maze Entrance.

The basic rule of this maze is that there is some information on every web page, and parsing this information to get plaintext

(plain text is readable in Chinese or English)

Enter "plaintext .html" into the last part of the address bar to enter the lower layer of the maze

Example: The address of this page is

https://hydr00.github.io/Safepedia/Maze/Mazestart.html , Plaintext is level2

Then enter in the address bar https://hydr00.github.io/Safepedia/Maze/level2.html You can get to the next maze.
